Why Choose Upscale Care?

With our Care service, a fully-trained care assistant will come to your door each day and provide you or your loved one with the support you require.

  • We will provide a personalised care plan that we’ll draw up together
  • Flexible visiting schedule from once or twice a week to four visits a day.
  • Care visits from fifteen minutes up to three hours at a time
  • You tell us when you can manage certain tasks and when you want us to assist you!

Our Mission

We aim to improve lives, one home at a time, through outstanding domiciliary care and assisted living services. We are committed to offering the utmost care and kindness, contributing to the physical, emotional, and mental well-being of our clients.

Key Aims and Objectives

  • Client-Centred Care: We work closely with clients, their families, and support networks to develop comprehensive care plans, continually reviewing and adapting them to ensure evolving needs are met.
  • Fairness and Respect: Our staff are trained to interact with clients respectfully, recognising their inherent worth and honouring their cultural backgrounds
  • Upholding Ethical Standards: We rigorously protect client privacy and dignity across all our services.
  • Health and Well-Being Focus: By working with healthcare professionals, we ensure coordinated care that supports clients' physical and mental health. We actively monitor and improve overall health outcomes.
