Why Choose Upscale Care?

With our Care service, a fully-trained care assistant will come to your door each day and provide you or your loved one with the support you require.

  • We will provide a personalised care plan that we’ll draw up together
  • Flexible visiting schedule from once or twice a week to four visits a day.
  • Care visits from fifteen minutes up to three hours at a time
  • You tell us when you can manage certain tasks and when you want us to assist you!
